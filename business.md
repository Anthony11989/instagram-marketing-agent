# Business profile

Edit everything below to describe your business. Claude reads this file every
time it writes a caption, so the more specific and honest you are here, the
better the captions fit your brand. Plain sentences are fine.

## What we are
(Example: A neighborhood Italian restaurant in downtown Phoenix focused on
handmade pasta and wood-fired dishes, open for dinner Tuesday through Sunday.)

## Our vibe and voice
(Example: Warm, unpretentious, a little playful. We talk like a friendly host,
never corporate. We avoid overused words like "elevate" and "indulge.")

## What we want people to do
(Example: Book a table through the link in our bio, come in for happy hour
4 to 6pm, or order takeout for pickup.)

## Who we are talking to
(Example: Locals aged 25 to 55, date-night couples, families on weekends,
and downtown workers looking for a quick quality lunch.)

## Things to always do
- Mention the neighborhood or city when it fits.
- Keep captions short and skimmable.
- Use 3 to 5 relevant, specific hashtags on the last line.

## Things to never do
- Never invent menu items, prices, or events that are not in the post direction.
- Never claim awards or reviews we have not actually received.
- No emojis unless the direction asks for them.
