# Business profile

XL for Hospitality (Excel for Hospitality & Consulting LLC) makes BarLedger, an
inventory and cost-control system for bars and restaurants. This file guides every
Instagram caption and overlay.

Voice: write as the company ("we"), clean and practical, operators talking to
operators, never corporate, no hype words, and no emojis.

## What we are
We are hospitality operators who build the systems venues actually need. Our flagship
product is BarLedger, the liquor, beer, and wine (LBW) inventory system. We also consult 
on staff systems, financial auditing, process optimization, menu engineering, and growth 
strategy. Everything comes from real floor experience, not theory. We sell worldwide.

## What BarLedger does
- Counts liquor, wine, beer, and non-alcoholic stock fast with barcode scanning
- Runs on your local network with a mobile scanner
- Tracks breakage, waste, monthly COGS, and variance
- Exports clean PDF and data reports
- Costs custom and batched cocktails so you know your true pour cost
- POS CSV import (Toast, Square) and multi-location support on higher plans (not yet implimented but in development)

## Who we are talking to
Bar and restaurant owners, general managers, beverage directors, and multi-venue groups
and hotels. People tired of losing time and money to bad inventory.

## Problems we solve (lead with these)
- Hours lost counting bottles and writing down by hand
- Shrinkage, theft, and unexplained variance eating into profit
- Not knowing true pour cost or COGS
- Messy spreadsheets that fall apart across shifts and locations

## How the trial and billing work
Every plan starts with a 14-day grace period. After buying the activation code, the
first 14 days are free; the first charge is then prorated for the remaining days of that
first month, and the regular monthly rate applies from the next month on. Annual billing
means you pay for 10 months and get 12 months of access (2 months free).

## Pricing (state accurately, never invent numbers)
- Starter: $99/mo, or $990/yr (about $83/mo) on annual. One location. $500 one-time
  onboarding. Liquor/wine/beer/NA inventory, barcode and QR scanning, PDF and export
  reports, breakage and waste tracking, monthly COGS analysis, email support.
- Pro (most popular): $149/mo, or $1,490/yr (about $124/mo) on annual. Up to 3
  locations. $500 one-time onboarding. Everything in Starter plus POS CSV import
  (Toast, Square), LAN client mode, network and NAS storage, priority email and phone
  support, auto-updates, checkbook and vendor tracking.
- Enterprise: custom pricing, annual contract, onboarding included. Unlimited locations,
  everything in Pro plus custom POS integration, dedicated onboarding and training, an
  SLA and dedicated support line, multi-venue reporting, custom feature requests.

## What we want people to do (CTA)
Drive people to the BarLedger page, which is linked in our bio, to get started or learn
more. Default CTA: "Get started, your first 14 days are free. Link in bio." A good
alternative: "Book a free demo, link in bio."
